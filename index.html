overview

Takaaki Fukuda is a web designer/developer in Japan. His web career began in Canada, where he studied Web Development & Interactive Design at Vancouver Institute of Arts. During this time, he also started his professional career as a freelancer, designing and developing websites for local artists in Vancouver, BC. He graduated as one of the top students at VanArts.

In his spare time, he always learns new web technologies.Takaaki is also an active traveler, professional DJ for Abysmal Lounge, coffee enthusiast, and a good friend to many people around the world.
